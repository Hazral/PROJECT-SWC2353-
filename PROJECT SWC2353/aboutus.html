Our Mission

Empowering Communities, Transforming Lives

The goal of African Fundraiser is to be a social justice organization that supports developing African communities projects. We act on education, health, and economic inclusion to respond to the needs of the most disadvantaged areas.

Through construction of schools as well as offering scholarships and vocational training we make education a reality, the key to better and brighter future. Specifically within healthcare we address access to vital care services, prevention and other measures that better health in communities.

We strive to increase the economic self sufficiency of the African people, promoting business development and marketable ventures. We come from partnerships and community-based initiatives to work together in order to end poverty and build sustainable development.
